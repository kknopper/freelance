TPTS001 - Insights from the “Rudy of TCU” on how he walked on at a nationally ranked program and landed a job as a TV news anchor all by the age of 22.

Posted by: Tim O'Brien

Show notes

At 5’ 7’’, 150 pounds few gave Robbie Fuelling a chance to play football for the nationally ranked, TCU Horned Frogs. But then again, Robbie has never been the type of person to wait for others to call his number.

In this action-packed interview, Robbie shares some excellent insights on reputation management, including some very specific strategies he utilized to earn the trust and affection of All-American teammates. Robbie also gives us an inside look at how he leveraged his experiences on the field into a job as a news anchor for the ABC Affiliate, KEZI News in Eugene, Oregon.

If you want to learn more about how determination combined with a compelling personal brand can get you what you want, then this is one episode you do not want to miss.

Great takeaways include:

  • The importance of believing in yourself
  • Picking your brand and then living into it
  • Tips and strategies that will get you noticed
  • How just showing up sets you apart
  • Creating a plan and sticking to it
  • Preparing now for opportunities that will come later
