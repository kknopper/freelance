What is EPIC Mastermind?

A strategic Board of Advisors composed of ten professionals/business owners dedicated to helping each other exponentially grow their businesses through the discovery and implementation of breakthrough strategies.

Group meetings involve an aggressive discussion of ideas that challenge members to look at their businesses differently and make the tough decisions that are necessary to break free of limiting behaviors and beliefs.

What is the mission of EPIC?

One thing and one thing only: Double members’ businesses.

Who is a good fit for EPIC?

Professionals/business owners who meet the following criteria:

  • Respond to coaching
  • Open to change
  • Professionally successful
  • Hungry to breakthrough to the next level
  • Embrace accountability

Who is not a good fit for EPIC?

EPIC is probably not for you if you:

  • View coaching as an expense vs. an investment
  • Are In the early stages of your career
  • Business Development is not a priority for yo
  • Don’t respond well to being challenged and held accountable

When and where does Epic meet?

EPIC meets once per month (2nd Tuesday of the month) from 2-6pm in downtown, Los Angeles.

Tim meets once-on-one with each member in between meetings to assist with implementation.

What does a typical meeting look like?

Sample Agenda:

First Hour-2:00-3:00pm

  • Catch up – Share one piece of good news unrelated to your business
  • Questions to reflect on prior to the meeting:
    • Update on the status of your business (Rate 1 to 10, what’s working, and what’s not working)
    • Outside of your immediate family who is the most empowering person(s) in your life right now?
    • What public figure do you admire the most and why?
    • What has been your most recent personal branding faux pas? Why and what was the impact
  • Update from Laine on his Commanding Presence workshop

Second Hour-3:10-4:00pm

  • Update on execution on Monthly Objectives (e.g. Why are you killing it? Why are you sucking?)
  • Back to the Basics - Standards for Success

Third Hour-4:10-5:00pm

  • Social Media Discussion with Travis Houston – TOB’s (and Wes’) Chief Digital Marketing Officer – Your Genius Model
  • Back to The Basics - Blockers

Fourth Hour- 5:10-6:00pm

  • Issue Discussion
  • Setting May’s Monthly Objectives
  • Final Thoughts & Rate the meeting

What makes EPIC different from other groups?

There are two principle differentiators: The focus of our meeting and the one-on-one coaching in between meetings.

Whereas other groups focus on organization, process and execution EPIC focuses exclusively on business development. What must happen for members to significantly grow their businesses in as short as time as possible? (e.g. 30% in the next 180 days).

Tim’s one-on-one clients pay him as much as $60,000 per year to coach them. You get access to Tim for fraction of that cost. Tim is a no nonsense, tells it like it is coach that has help thousands of clients just like you explode through to the next level.
