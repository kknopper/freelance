TPTS003 - Learn What it Takes to Be a Great Public Speaker from THE GUY who trained all of Anthony Robbins’ Peak Performance Coaches

Posted by: Tim O'Brien

How would you like me to let you listen in on my conversation with THE GUY who Tony Robbins himself asked to train ALL of his Peak Performance Coaches on the art of presenting?

Well, that is exactly what I have for you!

For six years, Roberto Monaco worked for the Anthony Robbins companies. From 2004-2007, he was the company’s top sales and revenue generator. Roberto was so good that in 2005, he was hand-picked by Tony Robbins to train all of the Anthony Robbins Companies Peak Performance Coaches on how to make effective presentations.

Roberto has delivered more than 4500 presentations in the United States, England, Canada, Mexico, Brazil and Puerto Rico.

In 2009, Roberto broke off on his own and started Influenceology, a presentation skills company that has helped literally thousands of professionals, entrepreneurs and business executives how to be great presenters.

Roberto is the founder of the acclaimed public speaking program, Influencing from the Front of the Room.

Great takeaways include:

  • The number one thing you must do to be a great presenter
  • How to structure your presentations so they have maximum impact
  • Why anyone can be an effective public speaker
  • Tips, techniques and strategies that will immediately help you be a better speaker
  • The key Do’s that must be included in every presentation
  • The Do Not’s that must be avoided at all costs
